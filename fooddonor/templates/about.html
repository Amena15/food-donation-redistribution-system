ABOUT US

Empowering Communities, Reducing Waste, Fighting Hunger.

OUR MISSION

At Share Food, Share Hope, our mission is to bridge the gap between food waste and hunger. We connect generous food donors with people in need through a secure, simple, and accessible platform across Malaysia.

OUR STORY

Founded in Malaysia, this platform was built with a vision of fostering unity through food sharing. With millions of tons of food wasted globally and thousands still going hungry, we believe it's time for change—starting at the community level.

OUR IMPACT

Every meal shared is a step closer to a hunger-free nation. From homes to restaurants, every contribution counts. Join us to create a lasting social and environmental impact today.

OUR TEAM

We are a passionate team of students, developers, volunteers, and advocates working together to make food more accessible and waste less impactful.
